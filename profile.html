Lucy Albright's Story

Lucy Albright is the main character in Tell Me Lies, both in the book by Carola Lovering and the Hulu series. Her story focuses on her childhood, her complicated relationships, and the impact of her intense relationship with Stephen DeMarco

Lucy's College Years

At Baird College, Lucy built friendships that helped her settle into college life but her relationship with Stephen quickly became the center of everything. Their bond was toxic, emotional and like a magnet, hard for her to walk away from. Her experiences in college shows how easy it is to lose yourself when you are trying to figure out who you are and what you want.

Key Relationships

  1. Stephen DeMarco- Her toxic romantic partner
  2. Pippa- Lucy's close friend, whose bond is tested with her over time
  3. Bree- A loyal friend who gave Lucy stability and perspective
  4. Diana- A classmate and another one of Stephen's entanglements

Major Themes in Lucy's story

  • Love and toxicity
  • Secrets and betrayal
  • Long term effects of first love
  • Self discovery
